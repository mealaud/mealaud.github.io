{{% MathEnv "nprop" %}}
One must never resign.
{{% /MathEnv %}}
{{% Proof %}}
If one resigns to their situation (i.e., gives up and no longer puts in effort into their life), [On Improving My Life]({{<ref "/axioms/on-improving-my-life">}}) tells us that their life can no longer neither markedly improve and stay improved. Therefore we are violating [Axiom \#4]({{<ref "/axioms">}}) and thus one cannot resign.
{{% /Proof %}}
