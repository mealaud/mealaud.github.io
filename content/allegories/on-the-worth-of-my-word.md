What is the worth of my word? I’d like to believe my word has worth, but does it? Why would I ever say such a thing?! Giving the air of doubt to something that should be so fundamental! Why? Though at conflict with what I would like to believe, I do not give my words value. I make empty promises to myself often and nothing comes of them (as one would expect). Why would I make those promises at all? Because I am under the guise that my word does have worth and that I give it the respect necessary for it to have value. That must change. I must truly value my word, my promises, my speech, and my acts following directly from speech. What is life but a promise to oneself to live? I must live. I must keep my word. I must keep my promise. I must keep my promises (to myself and others).
