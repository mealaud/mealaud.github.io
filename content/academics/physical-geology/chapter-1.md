### Preface
It seems that although philosophers of the ye old did think about the Earth quite a bit, they didn't really contribute anything to the development of geology. L. Rather, mining (like in _Mine_craft) posed practical problems which helped develop geology, but the authors emphasize that "the growth of [geology] to its present substantial proportions was largely the result of the intellectual curiosity of the human mind."
{{%nl%}}{{%nl%}}

Geology also uses seemingly all other physical sciences (e.g., physics, chemistry, astronomy, biology, etc.) to understand the Earth and its history. In fact, this coming section is about Earth and its place in the solar system.
