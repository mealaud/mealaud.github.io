### The Hahn-Banach Theorem

A big question one can pose for these seemingly pathological infinite-dimensional normed spaces is whether there are *any* nonzero bounded linear functionals on the space. In finite-dimensions its easy enough, but, as is often the case, infinite-dimensional spaces are a bit harder to deal with. The Hahn-Banach Theorem answers this question in the positive: there **are** bounded linear functionals.
