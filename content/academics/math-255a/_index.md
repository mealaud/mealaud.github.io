These are my notes based on my Math 255A (Functional Analysis) class taught by Prof. Michael Hitrik during UCLA's 2024 winter quarter. An important thing to note is the philosophy of this class (per Prof. Hitrik): we will be learning functional analyis as a tool for other areas of analysis (in particular, PDEs), i.e., we will learn theory but mainly focus on the applications.

My main references have been the following three sources

1. Lectures by Michael Hitrik. (cf. [Daniel Raban's lecture notes](https://pillowmath.github.io/Math%20255A/main.pdf))
2. *Real Analysis: Modern Techniques and their Applications* by Gerald Folland.
3. *Functional Analysis* by Walter Rudin.

Any additional references and related readings will be cited at the end of the post, though it should be assumed that *all* posts in this project are based on the above three references.
