We know from {{<ref "the Moral of Manifolds" "/notes/the-moral-of-manifolds">}} and {{<ref "Definition of a Topological Manifold" "/notes/definition-of-a-topological-manifold">}}, that manifolds are locally like Euclidean space. Now, put this in your pipe and smoke it: 
1. differentiation makes sense in Euclidean space;
2. differentiation is a local property; and
3. manifolds are locally like Euclidean space.
