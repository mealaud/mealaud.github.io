The win condition or {{% tdf "payoff" %}}  of a game is the value that determines who has won the game. Practically, the payoff is defined on a game-by-game basis. For example, in tag you could set the win condition to be based on who lasts longer as the evader. Where as in rock-paper-scissors you could say the a player wins if they win two of three rounds of the game.{{%nl%}}{{%nl%}}

I think it's also neat that these examples are a game of degree and a game of a kind, respectively. (cf. {{<ref "Types of Games" "/notes/types-of-games">}}.)
