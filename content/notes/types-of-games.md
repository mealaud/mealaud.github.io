There are two types of games: _games of a kind_ and _games of degree_.
- {{% tdf "Games of a kind" %}} are games where there are only finitely-many outcomes.
- {{% tdf "Games of degree" %}} are games where they is a continuum of outcomes. 

Here is an example.
{{% MathEnv "ex" %}}

A game of a kind would be something like rock paper scissors. A game of degree would be something like tag, but where we choose the winner by how long they evade capture.
