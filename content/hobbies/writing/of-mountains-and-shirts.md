The mountain is immovable.{{%nl%}}
It stands strongly despite all it endures.{{%nl%}}
Its clothes may be ragged but it continues to do what it must.{{%nl%}}{{%nl%}}

May I be a mountain.{{%nl%}}
May my shirt have holes.
