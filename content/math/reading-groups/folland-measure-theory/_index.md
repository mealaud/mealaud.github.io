With my friends Hunter and Kevin, we covered the first four chapters of Folland's Measure Theory text last summer (summer 2022) and it was really fun! We learned a whole lot of stuff. Because of this and our desire to learn more analysis (!), Hunter and I have decided to continue our adventure and keep reading. We're hoping to cover Chapters 5, 6, and 7 this summer. 

## Pre-requisites

Since we're picking up where we left off, we are assuming familiarity with chapters 1-4 of Folland.

## References

We are using Gerald B. Folland's *Real Analysis Modern Techniques and Their Applications* for material and Terry Tao's [Math 245B](https://www.math.ucla.edu/~tao/245b.1.04w/) and [Math 245C](https://www.math.ucla.edu/~tao/245c.1.05s/) courses as references for what exericses to do.
