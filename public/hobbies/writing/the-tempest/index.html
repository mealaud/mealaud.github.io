The Tempest Saturday, October 6, 2018

The tempest erupts once again and everything is re-enlivened.

The beings of the old calibrate to the changing times, their roots rearrange and change, their leaves vibrate in the unrelenting winds, while their trunks stand strong in the force of powers greater than itself.

The tree that guards its underlings curls over to these winds. He forces the direction of the curl to move in a way such that his children are protected.

Even in the most tumultuous of tempests, he will cover his subordinates and continue on for another day.

The storm subsides.
