On the Worth of My Word Tuesday, April 2, 2024

What is the worth of my word? Iâ€™d like to believe my word has worth, but does it?

Though at conflict with what I would like to believe, I do not give my words value. I make empty promises to myself often and nothing comes of them (as one would expect). Why would I make those promises at all? Because I am under the guise that my word does have worth and that I give it the respect necessary for it to have value. That must change.

I must truly value my promises and act to corroborate this fact-to-be.

Moreover, what is life but a promise to oneself to live? And I must live.
