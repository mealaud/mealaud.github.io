On the Worth of My Word Wednesday, August 9, 2023

My word must have worth and, if it does not, I can ensure it will.
I have made many empty promises to myself in the past (whether I was aware or not). Each time I became aware of a such a lie I doubt my ability to change, to improve, to continue. This must not be possible. My word must have worth so that I can keep promises to myself (and to others).

We suppose without loss of generality that my word does not have worth. (As if it did we would be done!) In this case, what can be done to amend it? Like

states, this can only improve if I make a continuous effort, hence I will suppose my word does and act to make this true.

In some sense, it is irrelevant whether your word means anything as you must act like it does to give it the hoped for worth. Roughly speaking, this is “fake it till you make it”.
